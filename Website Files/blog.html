Step-Up Exercises: The Top Performer

The step-up exercise, along with its variations—lateral step-up, diagonal step-up, and cross-over step-up—emerged as the most effective movement for maximizing GMax activation...

Hip Thrusts, Deadlifts, and Squats: Close Contenders

Factors Influencing Glute Activation

  • External Load: Using heavier weights typically leads to greater GMax activation...
  • Movement Velocity: The speed at which an exercise is performed can affect muscle recruitment...
  • Stabilization Requirements: Exercises that require greater stability tend to increase GMax activation...

Practical Recommendations for Glute Training

  1. Incorporate a Variety of Movements: Include a mix of unilateral exercises...
  2. Focus on Stabilization: Exercises that challenge stability can increase GMax activation...
  3. Adjust Your Squat Technique: Experiment with different squat variations...
  4. Use Progressive Overload: Gradually increase the load or complexity of your exercises...
